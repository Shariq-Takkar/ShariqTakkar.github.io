VIPER



Yash Soni is an Indian professional E-sports player, streamer, live commentator, gaming creator, and social media influencer from Mumbai, Maharastra, India. He is popularly known as a Soul Viper since he started playing PUBG Mobile on Youtube.
Besides these, he is the oldest member of Soul Clan along with his best friend Soul Mortal and Soul Regaltos. The team has won "PMPL South Asia Team 2020" and the team qualified directly to the Global Finals of PUBG Mobile Club Open Fall Split 2019 as one of South Asia's top-scoring teams bagging the 2nd position.
Currently, Soul Viper and his team are one of the most popular gamers in India and outside India. They all represented India at the forefront of the world through playing PUBG Mobile. His gaming channel is one of the most popular E-Sports and PUBG Mobile gameplay channels in India.
