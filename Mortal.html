MORTAL


Naman Mathur, popularly known as Soul Mortal, is an Indian Gamer and YouTuber who has gained widespread recognition for his exceptional skills in playing the mobile game PUBG Mobile. Soul Mortal began his gaming career in 2018 by playing PUBG Mobile. He created a YouTube channel named Mortal, where he started uploading videos of his gameplay. With time, his gaming skills and content gained a massive following, and he became one of the most popular gamers in India.
In 2019, Soul Mortal and his team Soul won the PUBG Mobile India Series, which was a turning point in his career.
Team Soul Won PUBG Mobile Club Open Spring Split India Regional Finals. Following his victory in the PMCO 2019 gaming tournament, Soul Mortal demonstrated his philanthropic spirit by donating the entirety of his prize money to the Indian Army.
