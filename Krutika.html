KRUTIKA



Krutika Plays real name is Krutika Ojha, an Indian gamer, streamer, digital content creator, and social media influencer from Maharashtra. Krutika Ojha was born on 6th March 2000 in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India into a Hindu family. She is 22 years old as of 2022. She has quickly become one of the leading faces in the world of female Indian gamers. She began her gaming career by uploading her first gaming video on Instagram on 25th June 2020. Five days later, she appeared on YouTube and posted her first video there titled “MY PMIS JOURNEY” on 30th June 2020. The video received appreciation from gamers and viewers alike and this gave Krutika motivation to make gaming into a career. Her video “20k Subs Celebration Gone Wrong ft. @8bit MAMBA” was the first to go viral and to date, it has over 358kviews on YouTube. On 13th July 2021, she posted a video titled “The Great MAMBA *ROASTED *”, which made her a familiar face in the world of FPS gaming as it received a million views in a matter of months. This video featured famous gamers like 8bit MAMBA and PAYAL GAMING. After that, the number of her fans and followers spiked and she got famous on social media.
